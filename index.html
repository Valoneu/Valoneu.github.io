About Me

Hey there! I'm a Computer Science student at CVUT FIT in Prague. I enjoy combining technology with creative work, focusing on coding, 3D modeling, and game modding.

I program in C++, C#, Java, JavaScript, Lua and Python, and have some experience with databases. For art, I use Blender for 3D projects and Aseprite for pixel art. I particularly like modding games like Factorio and Unity games. Building interfaces with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript is also something I enjoy.

I'm always trying to learn more in both programming and art. This website shows some of my work.
