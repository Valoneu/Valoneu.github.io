About Me

I'm currently studying Computer Science at CVUT FIT in Prague. I love learning about new technologies and their applications in the realm of coding and art. I spend my free time gaming, modding, making 3d and making whatever code doodles i want. I primarly code in C++ and some JavaScript and Java, I know PSQL and some Python, but I am not a pro at any of them. In 3D i use Blender for modelling and rendering i have interest in hard surface modeling a modelling real life objects, and Aseprite for pixel art. I also dabble in modding for Unity games and Factorio. I'm always eager to learn about interesting subjects and imprive my skills in programming and 3D modeling.

When I'm not coding or creating, you can find me playing games, exploring new mods, or working on my next project. I also enjoy sharing my journey and insights through my blog.
